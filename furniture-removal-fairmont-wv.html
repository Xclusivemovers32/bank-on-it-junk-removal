Frequently Asked Questions

What kinds of furniture do you take?

We haul sofas, mattresses, bedroom sets, dining tables, bookcases, office desks and more.

Can you move furniture from upstairs?

Yes. Our team is trained to safely navigate stairs and tight hallways.

Do you recycle items?

Whenever possible. Usable items are donated to Fairmont area charities; broken pieces are sent to proper recycling centers.

How soon can you come?

Many jobs can be scheduled next‑day; same‑day pickups may be available.

How much does it cost?

Pricing is based on volume and effort. We’ll give you a firm quote before hauling anything away.

Do I need to prepare the furniture?

No — but please clear pathways and remove personal items from drawers.

Do you dismantle larger items?

Yes. Sectionals, wardrobes or bunk beds can be taken apart for safe removal.

Will you clean up afterwards?

Absolutely. We sweep the area and leave it tidy.
