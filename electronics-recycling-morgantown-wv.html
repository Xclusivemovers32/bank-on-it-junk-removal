Electronics Recycling in Morgantown, WV

Electronic waste is hazardous when dumped in landfills. BankOnIt Junk Removal partners with certified recyclers to offer responsible electronics recycling in Morgantown. From old TVs and computers to printers and small devices, we ensure your e‑waste is handled properly.

Services We Provide

  • TV and monitor removal
  • Computer and laptop recycling
  • Printer and fax machine disposal
  • Small electronics and battery pickup

Our Process

  1. Call or text to request an e‑waste pickup.
  2. We quote based on quantity and weight.
  3. Crew collects items and loads them securely.
  4. Electronics are delivered to certified recycling centers that wipe data and recycle components.

Pricing Factors

  • Number and type of devices
  • Data destruction needs
  • Hazardous component handling (CRT vs. LCD)
  • Floor access and loading effort

Why Choose Us

  • Data security: devices are destroyed or wiped
  • Compliance with e‑waste regulations
  • Partnered with certified recyclers
  • Fully insured crew
