Garage Cleanouts in Bridgeport, WV

Is your garage overflowing with boxes, tools and unwanted junk? We help Bridgeport residents reclaim their space. BankOnIt Junk Removal brings the muscle, organization, and hauling to clear garages quickly so you can park your car again or turn the space into a workshop.

Services We Provide

  • Full garage cleanouts (sort, haul, dispose)
  • Bulk trash and junk removal
  • Tool, paint and hazardous waste guidance

Our Process

  1. Walk‑through to identify what stays and what goes.
  2. We separate recyclables, donations and trash.
  3. Items are loaded, swept, and hauled away.
  4. Hazardous materials (e.g., old paint) are advised for proper disposal.
