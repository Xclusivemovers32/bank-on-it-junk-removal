Construction Debris Removal in Bridgeport, WV

Contractors and DIYers need a reliable partner to haul away renovation debris. BankOnIt Junk Removal provides quick, safe construction debris removal services in Bridgeport. From drywall and lumber to old cabinets and tiles, we keep job sites clean and compliant.

Services We Provide

  • Renovation debris pickup (drywall, lumber, flooring)
  • Demolition material hauling
  • Job‑site cleanup and sweeping
  • Flexible scheduling around your project timeline

Our Process

  1. Call for an on‑site volume estimate.
  2. We provide a quote and schedule pickups as needed.
  3. Our crew loads debris and sweeps the site.
  4. Materials are taken to the appropriate landfill or recycling facility.

Pricing Factors

  • Volume of debris (cubic yards)
  • Material type (concrete, wood, mixed)
  • Loading labor (distance to truck)
  • Multiple trips vs. single haul
