Hot Tub Removal in Morgantown, WV

Replacing a broken or unused hot tub? BankOnIt Junk Removal specializes in safe hot tub removal for Morgantown homeowners. We handle the electrical disconnection, draining, cutting and hauling so you don’t have to break a sweat.

Services We Provide

  • Hot tub and spa dismantling
  • Electrical disconnection assistance
  • Water draining and pump removal
  • Debris hauling and site cleanup

Our Process

  1. Inspect and disconnect power (with electrician assistance if needed).
  2. Drain the tub and disconnect pumps and jets.
  3. Cut the shell into manageable pieces.
  4. Remove decking or panels as needed.
  5. Load pieces and haul to recycling/disposal facility.

Pricing Factors

  • Tub size and access (backyard vs. deck)
  • Electrical complexity
  • Need to remove fencing or decking
  • Labor hours

Why Choose Us

  • Skilled removal crew; licensed and insured
  • Proper disposal of fiberglass and pumps
  • Respect for your landscaping and property
  • Written estimate with no surprises

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you handle electrical work?

We coordinate with a licensed electrician to disconnect power safely.

Can you remove indoor hot tubs?

Yes, as long as there is a clear path to exit.

What about the water?

We drain and recycle water responsibly.

Do you rebuild decks afterwards?

We remove decking to access the tub but do not rebuild; we can refer contractors.

How long does removal take?

Most hot tubs are gone in 2–4 hours.

Do you take swim spas?

Yes, though they require more labor and may need a crane.

How much does it cost?

Pricing depends on size and complexity; we provide a quote in advance.

How do I schedule?

Call, text or book online.
