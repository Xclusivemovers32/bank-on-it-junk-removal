Shed Demolition in Fairmont, WV

An old shed can be an eyesore and a safety hazard. BankOnIt Junk Removal offers professional shed demolition and removal throughout Fairmont. We handle the teardown, hauling, and clean‑up so you can reclaim your backyard.

Services We Provide

  • Full dismantling of wooden and metal sheds
  • Safe disposal of roofing, lumber and hardware
  • Site cleanup and debris removal
  • Optional foundation removal

Our Process

  1. On‑site assessment and quote for demolition.
  2. Disconnect utilities (if present) and prepare the area.
  3. Tear down the shed safely, piece by piece.
  4. Haul debris and sweep up nails and screws.
  5. Optional site grading or foundation removal.

Pricing Factors

  • Shed size and construction (wood vs. metal)
  • Presence of electricity or plumbing
  • Foundation removal needs
  • Hazardous materials (asbestos siding etc.)

Why Choose Us

  • Experienced crew with demolition training
  • Proper disposal at Fairmont transfer stations
  • Fully insured for demolition work
  • Transparent quotes and permits assistance

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a permit?

Some jurisdictions require a demo permit; we’ll advise and assist.

Can you remove concrete slabs?

Yes, for an additional fee.

What happens to the materials?

Wood, metal and roofing are recycled or disposed at approved facilities.

How long does demolition take?

Most sheds are removed in 3–5 hours.

Do you disconnect power?

We will safely disconnect electric lines; gas lines require a licensed professional.

Can you remove other structures?

We also demo decks, pergolas and playsets.

Is site grading included?

Light grading is available upon request.
